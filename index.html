    Rules

    Objective: The goal of Blackjack is to beat the dealer's hand without exceeding a total of 21. Players compete to get closer to 21 than the dealer while avoiding going "bust" (exceeding 21).

    How to Play:
    - Each player starts with $5000 and places a minimum bet of $50 per round.
    - Players are dealt two cards face-up, while the dealer receives one card face-up and one face-down.
    - Players can choose to "Hit" (take another card) or "Stand" (keep their current hand). The dealer must "Hit" if their hand total is less than 17 and "Stand" on 17 or higher.

    Winning and Losing:
    - Win if your hand total is closer to 21 than the dealer's without exceeding 21.
    - Lose if your total exceeds 21 or the dealer has a higher hand without busting.
    - Blackjack (Ace + 10-value card on the initial deal) pays 3:2.
    - A tie ("Standoff") results in the return of your bet.
